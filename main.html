About Us

The first international Conference on Artificial Intelligence, Smart Technology, and Communications (AISTC'25) aims to bring together leading academic scientists, international researchers, and professionals to share and exchange their experiences and research studies on all aspects of artificial intelligence. It also offers a premier interdisciplinary forum for researchers, professionals, and students to present and address the most current innovations, trends, concerns, and practical challenges confronted in the fields of artificial intelligence, as well as solutions adopted.

Submission

Authors are encouraged to submit papers in English of up to 12-15+ pages (400 words) for Full papers and 6-11 pages (400 words) for Short papers. Please find a template here. To submit their papers, authors may use the online submission system hosted by CMT Conference System; the URL is provided below. Only papers that have not previously been published or delivered will be accepted for AISTC'25'2024.
